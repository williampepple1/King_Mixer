Installation Guide

1

Windows — Installer

Run KingMixerInstaller.exe and follow the wizard. The VST3 plugin is installed to your system VST3 directory automatically.

2

Windows — ZIP

Extract KingMixer_VST3.zip and copy the King Mixer.vst3 folder to C:\Program Files\Common Files\VST3\.

3

macOS

Extract KingMixer_macOS_VST3.zip and copy the King Mixer.vst3 folder to ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/.

4

Rescan in your DAW

Open your DAW and rescan for new plugins. King Mixer will appear under your VST3 instruments or effects list.
